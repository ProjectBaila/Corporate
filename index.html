Frequently Asked Questions

Do participants need dance experience or fitness levels?

Absolutely not! Our programs are designed for all bodies and fitness levels. No dance skills, coordination, or athletic ability required. Movement is gentle, guided, and completely adaptable to each participant's comfort level.

Can these sessions be done virtually?

Yes! We offer in-person, virtual, and hybrid formats. Our virtual sessions are specially designed for remote teams and work seamlessly over video platforms like Zoom or Teams.

What space and equipment do we need?

Minimal requirements: an open space where participants can stand and move comfortably (conference room works fine), and a sound system for music. For virtual sessions, just a small area to stand. We bring everything else!

How do you measure the success of sessions?

We provide pre/post session feedback surveys, engagement metrics, and can track specific outcomes like stress levels, team cohesion scores, and energy improvements. Many clients also report measurable improvements in collaboration and creativity.

Can you accommodate large teams?

Yes! We work with teams from 5 to 200+ people. For large groups, we can use breakout sessions or auditorium-style facilitation depending on your goals and space.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary 15-20 minute discovery sessions so your team can experience our approach before committing to a full program. This helps ensure it's the right fit for your culture.
